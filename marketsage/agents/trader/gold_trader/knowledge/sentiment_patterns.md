# Gold Mining Sentiment Patterns

## Bullish Signals
- Insider buying mentions (especially warrant exercises settled in cash)
- Drilling superlatives ("best intercept ever", "world-class", "bonanza")
- Institutional involvement (Sprott, EdgePoint, Rick Rule mentions)
- Production milestone anticipation ("first pour", "ramp-up")
- Comparisons to successful producers ("Fosterville", "Pretium")

## Bearish / FUD Signals
- AISC criticism without context (comparing development AISC to mature producers)
- "Pump and dump" accusations during normal price discovery
- Overemphasis on quarterly losses for pre-revenue companies
- Claims of management incompetence during standard orphan-period SP behavior

## Noise (Not Actionable)
- Price target speculation without supporting analysis
- Gold macro predictions (too many variables)
- Interpersonal conflicts between forum members
- Repeated posts by the same user
