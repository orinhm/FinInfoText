# Learnings


- [2026-04-27] Retail sentiment on platforms like CEO.CA can become heavily anchored to speculative future commodity prices (e.g., $4,000 gold), creating valuation targets ($7-$12/share) that are significantly decoupled from current spot realities.

- [2026-04-27] A key thematic driver in the Newfoundland gold sector is 'regional consolidation,' frequently identified through overlapping leadership roles, such as Denis Laviolette's involvement with both NFG and Sokoman (SIC).

- [2026-04-27] Logistical factors, such as the 170km haulage distance from deposit to mill, represent a critical bear-case variable (AISC inflation) that is often minimized in retail 'producer-math' discussions.

- [2026-04-27] CEO.CA is a specialized platform for junior mining and Canadian venture stocks and is an unreliable source for sentiment analysis of US mega-cap stocks like Tesla ($TSLA) due to low volume and high promotional noise.

- [2026-04-27] When scraping retail investment boards for large-cap tickers, a 'cross-tagging' filter is necessary to remove spam where penny stock promoters tag high-volume tickers to gain visibility.

- [2026-04-27] For mining industry valuation, NI 43-101 Technical Reports and Preliminary Economic Assessments (PEA) found on SEDAR+ are critical for verifying producer metrics like AISC, NPV, and metallurgical recovery rates.

- [2026-04-27] Enterprise Value per ounce (EV/oz) is a primary metric for peer benchmarking in the junior mining sector (e.g., comparing NFG to Sokoman or Calibre Mining).

- [2026-04-27] Form 51-102F1 (Management's Discussion & Analysis) is the essential filing for verifying actual cash-burn rates and treasury health for Canadian-listed mining companies.

- [2026-04-27] In junior mining analysis, retail sentiment on platforms like CEO.CA often focuses on 'short squeeze' narratives or market maker manipulation (e.g., Jane Street), which require validation via official IIROC/NYSE short interest reports.

- [2026-04-27] Provincial mineral land registries (e.g., Newfoundland & Labrador Mineral Lands Division) are the primary sources for verifying the status of environmental permits.

- [2026-04-27] Consumer sentiment data (e.g., FRED UMCSENT) often acts as a lagging or coincident indicator, making S&P 500 price action a necessary leading indicator for regime identification.

- [2026-04-27] Comprehensive risk-on/risk-off analysis requires tri-factor data coverage: Macro-economic health (FRED), Price/Volatility (Yahoo Finance), and Narrative/Sentiment (News/Fear & Greed Index).
