# Learnings


- [2026-04-28] [2026-04-28] Geopolitical shocks, such as the US-Iran deadlock on the Strait of Hormuz, can cause extreme algorithmic 'Fleece-Fire' spikes in silver futures (SI=F), pushing prices temporarily above $120/oz before stabilizing at a higher plateau (~$75/oz) compared to pre-crisis levels (~$48/oz).
