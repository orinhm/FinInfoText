# Learnings


- [2026-04-28] New Found Gold (NFGC / NFG.V) transitioned from pure explorer to producer in Q4 2025, recording its first revenue of $5.8M from the Hammerdown Mine and Pine Cove Mill. In April 2026, the company fully de-risked its Queensway Phase 1 path to production (targeted late 2027) by securing a $205M CAD finance package ($115M equity bought deal at $2.96 CAD and a $105M senior secured credit facility at 8.75%) backed by EdgePoint Investment Group and Eric Sprott, replacing a previously announced US$75M Nebari credit term sheet. Exploration continues to yield bonanza grades, including 71.8 g/t Au over 31.95m at Iceberg and extending the Dropkick Zone 11km north of the AFZ Core.
