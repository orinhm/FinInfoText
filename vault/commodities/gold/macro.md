# Learnings


- [2026-04-27] During extreme deleveraging events (margin call environments), gold can serve as a global liquidity source or 'ATM,' causing it to decline alongside risk assets despite its safe-haven status.

- [2026-04-27] Stablecoin issuers like Tether are evolving into pseudo-central banks by amassing significant physical gold reserves (e.g., 140+ tons), providing a psychological 'Sprott Shield' for digital assets.

- [2026-04-27] A BPGDM (Gold Miners Bullish Percent Index) reading below 20 is a historically significant 'buy' signal for the gold mining sector, often preceding a major leg up.

- [2026-04-27] The integration of stablecoin liquidity (e.g., Tether) with physical gold wholesalers (e.g., Gold.com) is creating a new infrastructure for tokenized gold, facilitating an 'Individual Gold Standard' and digital liquidity for physical metal.
