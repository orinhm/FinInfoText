# Learnings


- [2026-04-28] Barrick Gold Corporation officially changed its name to Barrick Mining Corporation in May 2025. Concurrently, its NYSE ticker changed to 'B' (TSX: ABX.TO). The ticker 'GOLD' on Yahoo Finance now belongs to Gold.com, Inc. (formerly A-Mark Precious Metals, Inc. prior to December 2025).
