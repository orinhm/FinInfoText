# Learnings


- [2026-04-28] For mega-cap EV/Tech hybrids like Tesla, low operating margins (e.g., 4%) coupled with extreme P/E multiples (>300) confirm that the market has entirely detached the valuation from hardware manufacturing, pricing the asset almost exclusively on future software (FSD), robotics, and AI energy infrastructure.
