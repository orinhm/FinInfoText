# Learnings


- [2026-04-29] [2026-04-29] High debt-to-equity ratios in mature, prime defense contractors (e.g., LMT at 276%) are frequently the mathematical result of decades of aggressive share repurchases and consistent dividend returns that suppress book equity, rather than an indicator of structural over-leverage.

- [2026-04-29] [2026-04-29] Defense prime equities are exhibiting extreme sensitivity to U.S. political crosscurrents. Sharp sector-wide selloffs can occur when ambitious macro defense budgets (e.g., a $1.5 Trillion plan) face bipartisan legislative pushback or when shifting polling data suggests a less defense-favorable electoral outcome (e.g., 'Blue Wave' fears).
