# Learnings


- [2026-04-27] Market sentiment analysis for mega-cap equities is increasingly incorporating data from prediction markets (e.g., Polymarket) to gauge earnings 'beat' probabilities, supplementing traditional retail and institutional sentiment.

- [2026-04-27] A 'funding premium' is being applied to long-term projects as the reliability of US Treasuries as a stable 'risk-free rate' provider diminishes.

- [2026-04-27] The 'Risk-Off disguised as Risk-On' phenomenon occurs when equity indices remain stable because investors use them as a hedge against currency debasement rather than a bet on economic growth.
