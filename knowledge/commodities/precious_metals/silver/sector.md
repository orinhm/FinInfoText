# Learnings


- [2026-04-28] [2026-04-28] Geopolitical shocks, such as the US-Iran deadlock on the Strait of Hormuz, can cause extreme algorithmic 'Fleece-Fire' spikes in silver futures (SI=F), pushing prices temporarily above $120/oz before stabilizing at a higher plateau (~$75/oz) compared to pre-crisis levels (~$48/oz).

- [2026-05-04] During extreme geopolitical commodity spikes ('Fleece-Fire' events, e.g., Silver pushing past $120/oz), streaming companies like Wheaton Precious Metals (WPM) tend to capture lower upside beta (+30% over 6mo) compared to traditional primary miners like Pan American Silver (+53%) or Hecla Mining (+49%), validating the lower operational leverage and risk-dampening nature of the streaming business model during massive spot price appreciation.
