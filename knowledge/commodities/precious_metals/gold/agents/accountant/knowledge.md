# Learnings


- [2026-04-27] Gold is transitioning from a speculative safe-haven to a 'neutral reserve asset' as institutional trust in US Treasuries erodes due to high sovereign debt levels.

- [2026-05-06] For established gold producers, strong and growing free cash flow (FCF) is a primary driver of valuation and shareholder returns. Consistent FCF enables debt reduction, increased dividends, and share buybacks, leading to a re-rating by the market.
