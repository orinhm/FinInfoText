# Learnings — Accountant / Reits


- [2026-04-29] When analyzing REITs, traditional P/E ratios are often inflated and misleading due to heavy non-cash depreciation charges lowering GAAP Net Income. FFO (Funds From Operations) and AFFO (Adjusted FFO) are the standard performance metrics. Additionally, REIT valuations are highly sensitive to the 10-Year Treasury yield; tracking the 'yield spread' (REIT Dividend Yield minus 10-Year Yield) is essential for assessing relative value.
