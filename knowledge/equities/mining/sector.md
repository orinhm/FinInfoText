# Mining Equities — Sector Intelligence

Mining companies are equities that derive value from commodity extraction.

alongside equity analysis frameworks.

## Learnings

- [2026-04-27] New Found Gold Corp ($NFG) underwent a critical pivot in Q1-Q2 2026 from an 'exploration' phase to a 'financed developer' phase following a $205M financing package.

- [2026-05-04] The planned IPO of Barrick's North American unit (estimated >$60B valuation) illustrates a major industry trend: mega-cap miners are spinning off Tier-1 'safe jurisdiction' assets from their higher-risk international portfolios to command premium valuations and avoid the 'geopolitical discount'.
