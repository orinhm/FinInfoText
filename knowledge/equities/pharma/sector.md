# Learnings


- [2026-04-29] [2026-04-29] In the mega-cap pharma sector, massive revenue concentration in a singular breakthrough TAM (like GLP-1 weight loss) triggers strategic M&A into adjacent therapeutic areas (e.g., Oncology) as companies deploy windfall cash flows to diversify pipeline risk before competitor drug approvals compress their 'single-product' multiples.
