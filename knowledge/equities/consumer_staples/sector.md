# Learnings


- [2026-04-29] In consumer staples and discount retail, mega-cap defensive stocks (like Costco) often command structural valuation premiums (P/E > 50) that act as a 'quality tax.' This premium is sustained by high membership renewal rates, strong ROE, and resilient aggregate retail sales that decouple from declining consumer sentiment, as cost-conscious consumers consolidate spending into value-oriented wholesale channels during macroeconomic uncertainty.
